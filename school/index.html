UChicago STEM Education Curriculum

We put emphasis on entrepreneurship because it's not only about starting businesses; it's a mindset that encourages initiative, innovation, and the ability to navigate complex situations. Our goal is to imbue students with entrepreneurial thinking that can be applied to various life contexts, whether they go on to launch businesses or bring fresh perspectives to existing organizations. Through our entrepreneurship education, we are preparing students to be leaders, innovators, and problem solvers in the 21st century.

Empowering Innovators for the Future

Entrepreneurship education at Orion STEM Schools will be guided by the Disciplined Entrepreneurship framework. This robust methodology offers students a systematic and thoughtful approach to starting their own innovative ventures.

Design Thinking from Stanford University

We believe in providing our students with practical insights and experiences in entrepreneurship. Our programs involve mentorship from successful entrepreneurs and opportunities for students to interact with the business community, helping them understand the realities of starting and managing a business. This exposure to real-world entrepreneurial environments enhances their learning and prepares them for potential entrepreneurship paths in the future.

Medical Science Curriculum

The ultimate goal of our curriculum and teaching approach is to prepare students to solve real-world problems. By integrating their learning across multiple disciplines and applying it to project-based activities, students will develop the skills necessary to address the challenges and opportunities of the future.

Foster Changemakers of tomorrow

Drawing insights from MIT's rich legacy, we introduce students to the thrilling world of entrepreneurship. The future isn't just to be lived; it's to be shaped.

Shape Tomorrow

At Orion, STEM isn't just a subject—it's a world of endless exploration, where your child's curiosity shapes tomorrow with Deep Dive into STEM.

Offer World-class, forward-thinking education

Drawing from prestigious institutions and frameworks like UChicago, Fin Edge, Harvard’s Making Caring Common Project, Stanford’s Design Thinking, and MIT's Disciplined Entrepreneurship, Orion offers students a world-class, forward-thinking education with Progressive Curriculum Foundations.

Champion Causes

Our students are global citizens, taking strides in local communities and beyond, championing causes, and leaving indelible positive footprints with care and compassion for the community.
